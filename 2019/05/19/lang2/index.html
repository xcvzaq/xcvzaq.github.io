About Comments

Hello,

During the three days from May 14 to 16, my app received more than 20,000 malicious comments. Under normal circumstances, my app may have two or three comments per day. My app is a utilities application for image stitching, and the content of these malicious comments is mostly repeated, mostly about games and lottery, completely unrelated to my app.

After my analysis of these comments, I made the following guess: These malicious comments may come from competitors.

  1. The nicknames of these comments are mostly composed of random letters or Chinese, and have no practical meaning.

  2. After carefully reviewing thousands of comments, you will find that most reviews are repeated many times, and most of the comments are about games and lotteries. And in many comments, “malicious charge” are inserted at random locations in the comments, resulting in the semantics of the entire comment is not very smooth.

So, I think these comments are likely to be done automatically by the program, not manually by a large number of users.
